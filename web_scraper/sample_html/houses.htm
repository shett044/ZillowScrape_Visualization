    Zillow helps you find the newest Minneapolis real estate listings. There are currently 1,453 for sale listings in Minneapolis zip codes, including condos, bank owned homes, short sales, townhomes, duplexes, land and luxury listings. If you're looking to rent, check out our extensive list of luxury apartments, townhomes. We make it easy to find your dream home by filtering home types, price, and size. Filtering with keyword search is also possible, like "waterfront" or "maintenance free siding " homes in Minneapolis.
